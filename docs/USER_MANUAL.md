# ImpactX User Manual

## Introduction

Welcome to ImpactX, a revolutionary decentralized disaster response platform that leverages blockchain technology and artificial intelligence to ensure transparent, efficient, and accountable humanitarian aid distribution. This user manual will guide you through all the features and functionalities of the platform.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the ImpactX website at https://impactx.org
2. Click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Choose your user type:
   - **NGO Representative**: For organizations providing disaster relief
   - **Volunteer**: For individuals contributing to disaster response efforts
   - **Donor**: For those wishing to contribute financially
   - **Developer**: For technical contributors and integrators
4. Fill in the required information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Organization (if applicable)
5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your email
6. Complete your profile by adding:
   - Profile picture
   - Bio/description
   - Areas of expertise or interest
   - Preferred communication language

### Logging In

1. Go to https://impactx.org
2. Click on "Login" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and password
4. For added security, enable two-factor authentication in your account settings

## For NGOs and Volunteers

### Portal Access

After logging in as an NGO representative or volunteer, you'll be directed to your personalized portal where you can:

- Submit project proposals
- Upload evidence of disaster response activities
- Track project progress and funding status
- Communicate with other platform participants

### Submitting Claims

1. Navigate to the "Claims" section in your portal
2. Click "New Claim"
3. Fill in the claim details:
   - Project title and description
   - Disaster type and location
   - Estimated budget and timeline
   - Number of people affected/benefited
4. Upload supporting evidence:
   - Satellite imagery (before and after disaster)
   - Photos from the disaster site
   - IoT sensor data (if available)
   - Drone footage (if available)
   - Written reports and documentation
5. Submit your claim for verification

### Tracking Progress

The platform provides real-time tracking of your projects:

- **Verification Status**: See if your claim has been verified by our AI systems
- **Funding Status**: Track when and how much funding has been allocated
- **Impact Metrics**: View real-time data on people helped and resources distributed
- **Community Feedback**: See feedback from beneficiaries and other stakeholders

## For Donors

### Making Donations

1. Browse projects in the "Projects" section
2. Use filters to find projects that match your interests:
   - Disaster type
   - Geographic location
   - NGO implementing the project
   - Project status (new, funded, completed)
3. Click on a project to view details:
   - Project description and goals
   - Evidence of need and impact
   - Funding progress
   - Verification status
4. Click "Donate" to contribute:
   - Choose donation amount
   - Select currency (ALGO, USDC, or fiat)
   - Confirm donation details
   - Complete the transaction

### Impact Tracking

As a donor, you can track the impact of your contributions:

- **Real-time Updates**: Receive notifications about project progress
- **Verification Reports**: Access detailed reports on how funds were used
- **Beneficiary Stories**: Read stories from people who benefited from your donation
- **Impact Metrics**: View quantifiable results like people helped, resources distributed, etc.

### Tokenized Impact Credits

Donors receive tokenized impact credits for their contributions:

- **Proof-of-Impact NFTs**: Unique digital assets representing your contribution
- **Trading Marketplace**: Trade or collect impact credits
- **Portfolio Dashboard**: View all your impact investments in one place

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Login Problems**:
- Ensure you're using the correct email and password
- Check your spam folder for verification emails
- Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link

**Transaction Failures**:
- Verify you have sufficient funds in your wallet
- Check network connectivity
- Ensure you're using a supported wallet

**Verification Delays**:
- Large evidence files may take time to process
- High system load may cause delays
- Contact support if delays exceed 24 hours
