# ImpactX Token Economy

## Introduction

The ImpactX platform utilizes a comprehensive token economy designed to incentivize participation, ensure sustainable growth, and align the interests of all stakeholders in the disaster response ecosystem. This document details the token structure, distribution mechanisms, and economic incentives that drive the platform.

## Token Overview

### IMPACT Token

The IMPACT token is the primary utility token of the ImpactX platform:

- **Token Standard**: Algorand Standard Asset (ASA)
- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 IMPACT tokens
- **Decimals**: 6
- **Token ID**: [To be assigned upon deployment]

### Token Functions

1. **Governance**: Voting power in DAO decisions
2. **Staking**: Earning rewards through token staking
3. **Access**: Premium features and services on the platform
4. **Rewards**: Incentives for platform participation
5. **Payments**: Transaction fees and service payments

## Token Distribution

### Initial Allocation

The total token supply is allocated as follows:

- **Community Treasury**: 40% (400,000,000 tokens)
  - DAO governance and community initiatives
  - Liquidity provision for decentralized exchanges
  - Ecosystem development grants

- **Team and Advisors**: 20% (200,000,000 tokens)
  - 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
  - Performance-based unlock conditions
  - Subject to clawback provisions for misconduct

- **Strategic Partners**: 15% (150,000,000 tokens)
  - Partnerships with NGOs and humanitarian organizations
  - Technology and infrastructure providers
  - Government and institutional collaborations

- **Public Sale**: 15% (150,000,000 tokens)
  - Tiered allocation to ensure broad distribution
  - Lock-up periods for large investors
  - Discount structure for early participants

- **Foundation Reserve**: 10% (100,000,000 tokens)
  - Long-term platform sustainability
  - Research and development initiatives
  - Emergency response funding

### Distribution Timeline

- **TGE (Token Generation Event)**: 30% of tokens released
- **Month 6**: Additional 20% released
- **Month 12**: Additional 25% released
- **Month 18**: Additional 25% released

## Token Utility

### Governance Participation

IMPACT token holders can participate in platform governance:

- **Proposal Submission**: 1,000 IMPACT tokens required
- **Voting**: 1 token = 1 vote (with quadratic voting options)
- **Delegation**: Ability to delegate voting power to representatives
- **Referendums**: Special votes on critical platform decisions

### Staking Rewards

Token holders can stake IMPACT tokens to earn rewards:

- **Annual Percentage Yield (APY)**: 8-12% based on network conditions
- **Lock-up Periods**: 
  - 30 days: Base rewards
  - 90 days: 1.2x multiplier
  - 180 days: 1.5x multiplier
  - 365 days: 2x multiplier
- **Slashing Conditions**: 
  - Misconduct results in token penalties
  - Inactivity may reduce rewards over time

### Platform Access

IMPACT tokens provide access to premium platform features:

- **Enhanced Analytics**: Advanced data visualization tools
- **Priority Support**: Dedicated customer service channels
- **Exclusive Events**: Access to community events and AMAs
- **Early Access**: First access to new features and services

### Marketplace Benefits

Token holders receive benefits in the Impact Marketplace:

- **Trading Fee Discounts**: Up to 50% reduction in marketplace fees
- **Exclusive Listings**: Priority for tokenized impact credit listings
- **Revenue Sharing**: Percentage of marketplace transaction fees
- **Loyalty Rewards**: Additional tokens for frequent marketplace users

## Economic Incentives

### Participation Rewards

The platform provides rewards for various types of participation:

1. **Verification Activities**
   - Claim submission: 10-100 IMPACT tokens per verified claim
   - Evidence provision: 5-50 IMPACT tokens per evidence item
   - Peer review: 2-20 IMPACT tokens per review

2. **Donation Incentives**
   - Donation matching: 5% token match on ALGO donations
   - Milestone achievements: Bonus tokens for donation milestones
   - Recurring donations: Additional rewards for sustained giving

3. **Development Contributions**
   - Bug bounties: 100-1000 IMPACT tokens per verified bug fix
   - Feature development: 1000-10000 IMPACT tokens per accepted contribution
   - Documentation: 50-500 IMPACT tokens per documentation improvement

4. **Community Engagement**
   - Forum participation: 1-10 IMPACT tokens per valuable contribution
   - Event attendance: 25-100 IMPACT tokens per attended event
   - Mentorship: 100-500 IMPACT tokens per successful mentorship

### Impact Credits

Donors receive tokenized impact credits that can be traded or collected:

- **Proof-of-Impact NFTs**: Unique digital assets representing donations
- **Impact Scoring**: AI-generated impact scores for each contribution
- **Credit Trading**: Secondary market for buying and selling impact credits
- **Portfolio Management**: Dashboard for tracking impact investments

## Tokenomics

### Inflation and Deflation

The token economy balances inflationary and deflationary mechanisms:

**Inflationary Factors**:
- Staking rewards: 8-12% annual inflation
- Participation rewards: Variable based on platform activity
- Ecosystem grants: Quarterly token allocations

**Deflationary Factors**:
- Transaction fees: 0.1% fee burned on all transactions
- Marketplace fees: 2-5% of transaction value burned
- Governance fees: 1 IMPACT token fee for proposal submission
- Buybacks: Quarterly token buybacks using platform revenue

### Price Stability

Mechanisms to maintain token price stability:

- **Algorithmic Supply Adjustments**: Dynamic token minting based on demand
- **Reserve Management**: Foundation reserve for market stabilization
- **Liquidity Provision**: Incentives for liquidity providers on DEXs
- **Stablecoin Integration**: Integration with stablecoins for predictable pricing

## Revenue Model

### Platform Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction Fees**
   - 0.1% fee on all platform transactions
   - Collected in IMPACT tokens and burned
   - Revenue shared with validators and stakers

2. **Marketplace Commissions**
   - 2-5% commission on impact credit trades
   - Variable rates based on credit quality and impact score
   - Commission paid in IMPACT tokens

3. **Premium Subscriptions**
   - Monthly and annual subscription plans
   - Paid in IMPACT tokens or stablecoins
   - Subscription revenue used for platform development

4. **Data Services**
   - API access to disaster data and analytics
   - Tiered pricing based on usage volume
   - Payments in IMPACT tokens or fiat currency

### Revenue Distribution

Platform revenue is distributed as follows:

- **Token Buybacks**: 40% of revenue used for token buybacks
- **Staking Rewards**: 30% distributed to stakers as rewards
- **Development Fund**: 20% allocated to platform development
- **Community Treasury**: 10% for community initiatives and grants
