# Impact Marketplace Documentation

## Overview

The Impact Marketplace is a revolutionary component of the ImpactX platform that enables the tokenization and trading of humanitarian impact. This decentralized marketplace allows donors to receive Proof-of-Impact NFTs for their contributions, which can be collected, traded, or used to demonstrate their commitment to humanitarian causes.

## Marketplace Architecture

### Tokenized Impact Credits

The marketplace operates on a system of tokenized impact credits:

1. **Proof-of-Impact NFTs**
   - Unique digital assets representing specific humanitarian contributions
   - Immutable records of donation details and impact metrics
   - Verifiable on the Algorand blockchain
   - Transferable between users

2. **Impact Scoring System**
   - AI-generated impact scores based on multiple factors:
     - Number of people helped
     - Resources distributed
     - Verification confidence levels
     - Long-term sustainability metrics
   - Scores range from 1-100 with detailed breakdowns
   - Regular updates based on ongoing project monitoring

3. **Credit Categories**
   - **Emergency Response Credits**: For immediate disaster relief
   - **Recovery Credits**: For long-term reconstruction efforts
   - **Preparedness Credits**: For disaster preparedness initiatives
   - **Community Development Credits**: For ongoing community support

### Smart Contract Infrastructure

The marketplace is powered by a suite of smart contracts:

1. **Marketplace Contract**
   - Facilitates buying, selling, and trading of impact credits
   - Manages order books and transaction settlements
   - Enforces platform fees and royalty distributions
   - Handles dispute resolution mechanisms

2. **NFT Minting Contract**
   - Creates unique Proof-of-Impact NFTs for donations
   - Embeds impact data and verification information
   - Manages metadata standards and updates
   - Controls minting permissions and limits

3. **Royalty Distribution Contract**
   - Automatically distributes royalties to platform stakeholders
   - Implements progressive royalty structures
   - Handles secondary market transaction fees
   - Manages community treasury contributions

## Economic Model

### Tokenomics

The marketplace operates with a balanced economic model:

1. **Transaction Fees**
   - 2% fee on all primary market transactions
   - 5% fee on secondary market trades
   - Fees distributed between platform development and community treasury
   - Volume-based discounts for high-activity users

2. **Royalty Structure**
   - 10% royalty on secondary sales for original project beneficiaries
   - 5% royalty for platform contributors and validators
   - 2% contribution to community development fund
   - Progressive royalty reductions for high-impact projects

3. **Staking Incentives**
   - IMPACT token staking for marketplace benefits
   - Reduced fees for stakers
   - Early access to new credit listings
   - Exclusive auction participation

### Liquidity Management

Ensuring a healthy marketplace ecosystem:

1. **Market Making**
   - Automated market makers for popular credit categories
   - Liquidity incentives for market makers
   - Spread management for price stability
   - Arbitrage prevention mechanisms

2. **Price Discovery**
   - Auction mechanisms for unique impact credits
   - Dutch auction for time-sensitive credits
   - Fixed pricing for standard impact credits
   - Negotiated trades for high-value transactions

3. **Market Stability**
   - Circuit breakers for extreme price movements
   - Market manipulation detection and prevention
   - Collateral requirements for large trades
   - Insurance mechanisms for trade failures

## Conclusion
