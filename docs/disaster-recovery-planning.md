# Disaster Recovery Planning Guide

This document provides a comprehensive guide to disaster recovery planning, covering business continuity strategies, data protection, system restoration, and organizational resilience for the ImpactX disaster response platform.

## Overview

Disaster recovery planning is essential for ensuring the continuity of critical operations and the rapid restoration of services following disruptive events. This guide outlines the processes, procedures, and strategies necessary to maintain organizational resilience and service continuity in the face of various types of disasters.
