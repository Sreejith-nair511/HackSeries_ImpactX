# Disaster Recovery Planning

This document outlines the comprehensive disaster recovery planning framework implemented in the ImpactX platform, covering business continuity, data protection, system restoration, and organizational resilience strategies.

## Overview

Disaster recovery planning is essential for ensuring the continuity of critical operations and the rapid restoration of services following disruptive events. The ImpactX platform implements a comprehensive disaster recovery framework that addresses technology, processes, and people to ensure organizational resilience and service continuity.

## Conclusion

The ImpactX disaster recovery planning framework provides a comprehensive approach to ensuring organizational resilience and service continuity in the face of disruptive events. By implementing structured processes for business impact analysis, risk assessment, recovery strategies, and continuous improvement, this framework ensures that critical operations can continue and services can be rapidly restored following any type of disaster.

The integration of technology, adherence to standards, and commitment to continuous improvement create a robust foundation for disaster recovery that can adapt to evolving threats and changing organizational needs while maintaining focus on the ultimate goal of protecting the organization's mission, reputation, and stakeholder interests.
