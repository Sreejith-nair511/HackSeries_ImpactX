# User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to ImpactX, a revolutionary platform for transparent and verifiable humanitarian aid distribution. This guide will help you navigate the platform, understand its features, and make the most of its capabilities to contribute to global disaster relief efforts.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Navigate to [impactx.org](https://impactx.org)
   - Click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner

2. **Account Registration**
   - Enter your email address and create a strong password
   - Verify your email through the confirmation link sent to your inbox
   - Complete your profile with name and location information

3. **Identity Verification**
   - For donors: Complete KYC process for larger donations
   - For NGOs: Submit organizational documentation for verification
   - For beneficiaries: Provide community referral information

### Understanding the Dashboard

1. **Navigation Menu**
   - Home: Overview of active disasters and funding
   - Projects: Detailed view of ongoing initiatives
   - Portfolio: Your donation history and impact
   - Governance: Participate in platform decision-making
   - Resources: Educational materials and documentation

2. **Key Metrics Display**
   - Total funds distributed
   - People helped
   - Active projects
   - Your contribution impact

3. **Quick Actions**
   - Donate to current emergencies
   - View verification reports
   - Participate in governance votes
   - Access educational resources

## For Donors

### Making Donations

1. **Select a Cause**
   - Browse active disasters on the homepage
   - Filter by location, type, or urgency
   - Read detailed project descriptions

2. **Choose Donation Amount**
   - Select from suggested amounts or enter custom value
   - View estimated impact of your contribution
   - Choose donation frequency (one-time or recurring)

3. **Payment Processing**
   - Credit/debit card payments
   - Cryptocurrency donations (ETH, ALGO, MATIC)
   - Bank transfers for large donations
   - Tax receipt generation

### Tracking Your Impact

1. **Portfolio Overview**
   - See all your donations in one place
   - View real-time impact metrics
   - Access detailed verification reports

2. **Impact Notifications**
   - Email updates on project progress
   - Blockchain transaction confirmations
   - Verification completion alerts

3. **Tax Documentation**
   - Automated receipt generation
   - Year-end donation summaries
   - Compliance reporting for organizations

### Participating in Governance

1. **Voting on Proposals**
   - Review new project proposals
   - Vote with your IMPX tokens
   - Delegate voting power if preferred

2. **Submitting Proposals**
   - Identify unmet humanitarian needs
   - Create detailed project proposals
   - Engage with community feedback

3. **Earning Rewards**
   - Receive IMPX tokens for active participation
   - Gain reputation points for valuable contributions
   - Access premium platform features
