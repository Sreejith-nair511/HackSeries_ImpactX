## Introduction

ImpactX is a comprehensive disaster response platform designed to streamline coordination between NGOs, volunteers, developers, and government agencies during emergency situations. The platform leverages cutting-edge technologies including IoT sensors, drone surveillance, AI analytics, and blockchain-based governance to create a robust ecosystem for disaster management.

## Getting Started

### Registration Process
1. Visit the ImpactX platform website
2. Select your user role (NGO, Volunteer, Developer, or Partner)
3. Complete the registration form with required information
4. Verify your email address
5. Wait for account approval (for NGOs and Partners)

### Login Process
1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Complete two-factor authentication if enabled
4. Access your personalized dashboard
